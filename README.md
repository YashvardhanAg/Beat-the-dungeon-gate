Beat the Dungeon Gate is a fast-paced puzzle game that requires players to bridge the gap between quick reflexes and mental math. Numbers descend from the top of the screen, and the player must catch them in specific columns to reach a Target Score.

The challenge lies in the strategic use of the four operational zones:

Addition (+) and Subtraction (-) for incremental adjustments.

Multiplication (×) for rapid scaling.

Modulo (%) for precise score manipulation and resetting values.

To balance the difficulty, I implemented a "Shield" power-up that allows players to ignore the next five falling numbers, providing a vital window for repositioning during high-velocity sequences.
